Skerritt on Hannity

October 21, 8:00 PM EST: Watch the Interview

Watch Senator Skerritt chat with old friend Sean Hannity about his campaign, policy stances, and his next moves. Or, read the transcript of the interview.

Sean Hannity (SH): Hi John, good to have you on the show again, how are you?

John Skerritt (JS): Hey Sean, what's going on? How was your evening?

SH: So John, you've been traveling all over the northeast doing rallies and talking to voters. How was your recent visit to the white house?

JS: Let me tell you Sean, I went to visit those guys, and they're a bunch of knuckleheads. All those guys. These clowns have no idea what they're doing.

SH: You've been talking about government reform for a long time now, what do you think the primary issue facing Americans is right now?

JS: You know, it's all about the money. You know that, right? That's all they care about, all they care about is revenue coming in. That's no way to manage a government. They don't know what they're doing.

SH: What do you recommend your constituency to do?

JS: Look let's face it. I don't mean to be rude, but most of my voters are idiots. I'm sorry, but your average voter is not very intelligent. The best thing they can do is vote for me, while I still care. I've been a senator in Maine for over 35 years now, and my state pretty much manages itself. We don't need these clowns telling us what to do, they have no idea what they're doing.

SH: One of your main campaign points relates to health care, which people are claiming is the largest cost for most American families. What do you have to say to all those who can't afford to pay their medical bills, or that don't have health insurance?

JS: These people, they have no idea. When we were younger we had to work our asses off. These people who don't get health insurance are a bunch of idiots. I'm sorry, but they are. Or those complaining about their bills being too high - read your policy, you morons! Bunch of freeloaders. They have to just get a damn job and stop complaining. No one is going to hand anything to you. I say to them, "Get a job, and trust in Jesus."

SH: I mean don't they understand that healthcare is not free? You don't want to work for free, why would you expect doctors and nurses to work for free? I think there's an air of entitlement that's become too common among our population.

JS: Absolutely Sean, absolutely.

SH: Recently Senator Kamala Harris made mention that health care should be free for everyone. She's claiming that hospitals and doctors should be paid for by the taxpayers. Clearly she's promoting socialism. What do you think of that?

JS: That's bullshit. Total bullshit. You can't listen to her.

SH: Because of her connections to the muslim brotherhood?

JS: Well yeah. But also, I mean, she's a woman! All they do is talk. **Makes cat meow noises**

SH: We're hearing similar sentiments about the so-called "student loan crisis." You've spoken a lot about that, haven't you?"

JS: Oh don't even get me started on these dumbass milleneals...
